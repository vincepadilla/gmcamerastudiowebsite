Instax Printing

Learn More About Our Instax Printing

Capture your memories in style with our Instax Printing packages, perfect for adding a personal touch to your photos. Choose from three unique formats that suit your needs:

Instax Mini

  • Outer Outer Dimensions: 2.1" x 3.4"
  • Photo Dimensions: 1.8" x 2.4""
  • Aspect Ratio: 3:4

Ideal for wallet-sized photos, the Instax Mini format is compact and versatile—perfect for small albums, creative scrapbooks, or sharing with friends.

Instax Wide

  • Outer Outer Dimensions: 4" x 3.4"
  • Photo Dimensions: 3.6" x 2.4""
  • Aspect Ratio: 7:5

Go big with Instax Wide! These prints offer more space to fit group photos, scenic landscapes, or anything that deserves a broader view.

Instax Square

  • Outer Outer Dimensions: 2.8" x 3.4"
  • Photo Dimensions: 2.4" x 2.4""
  • Aspect Ratio: 1:1

For a modern touch, Instax Square gives you the perfect frame for symmetrical and balanced shots great for portraits and Instagram-worthy moments.
